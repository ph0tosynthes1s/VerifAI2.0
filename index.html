About Us


VerifAI is a project aimed at the development of a cutting-edge technology for searching and verifying information about individuals on the global network. With the ever-increasing amount of information available online, it has become increasingly difficult to accurately verify the authenticity of an individual's identity, making it a struggle for businesses, governments and organizations to reliably operate.

VerifAI's comprehensive approach to identity verification is a powerful tool for organizations that want to validate identities in the most efficient and complete way possible.
